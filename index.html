Group training

Group training is an ideal option for those who want to improve their running technique, prepare themselves for starts under the supervision of a coach and join the Running Community.
Duration - 60-80 minutes.
The club has over 100 members. In the warm season, an average of 30-40 people attend training sessions, in winter - 15-30. Club trains people of different ages, professions, and lifestyles who want to run a half-marathon/marathon for the first time, or improve their results at these distances.

About Us

The school was established in January 2016 to teach people how to run safely and without harming their health. The main task is to teach the technique of natural running.Here they offer an individual approach and video analysis of running technique, help prepare for distances, choose a start and choose running shoes.

In 2018, the club organized three trips abroad: to Limassol (Cyprus), Riga (Latvia) and Riva Del Garda (Italy).
